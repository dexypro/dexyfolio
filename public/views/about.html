me

Hi, my name is Dejan. I am a 32-year-old Full-Stack Developer. Both as a developer and in my everyday life, I strive for efficiency. I have serious passion for UI effects, animations and creating intuitive and dynamic user experiences. Further improving my knowledge in this field and adapting to new technologies never overwhelms me, yet presents an inexhaustible source of enjoyment.

I’ve always been a tech enthusiast, and since I got my first Pentium III at the age of 12 in early 2003, I've been curious about anything related to computers. My journey as a programmer began somewhere in 2010 when I became a developer on a World of Warcraft private server. My duties included improving game scripts and controlling the database. This enabled me to get a fairly solid understanding of C++ and SQL .

In time the WoW community moved on and I started looking for another platform on which I could express my creative ideas. I dabbled in several programming languages like Python and Lua for some time but eventually got around to learning Javascript and the whole palette of front-end technologies.

Other than web development, I’m also passionate about C# and game development in the Unity Engine . When I'm not coding, drawing vectors or pushing pixels, you'll find me playing guitar and singing or competitively playing online video games.
